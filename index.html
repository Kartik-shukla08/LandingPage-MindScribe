We Have Nothing to Hide

MindScribe is designed first for your emotional safety, then for your data safety. It is a place to be honest with yourself without worrying who might be listening.

Your entries, voice notes, and reports live only on your computer. They are processed on your device, encrypted, and never sent to a remote server.

MindScribe is for anyone who wants a private place to journal, unpack their day, or talk things through at their own pace. It can complement therapy, self-help, or your existing routines, but it is not a crisis service.
